THE COMPUTER UNION

The Computer Union (officially the Revolutionary Computer Union) is a large-scale northern communist republic. Established in 1945, it has been ruled by the PC Communist Party since its founding by Nooblordjjj, its first dictator. It is the main setting of Papers Pls.

HISTORY OF THE UNION

THE REVOLUTION

The Computer Union was born from the flames of the Tsardom of Trollsa. The imperial government was thought to be greedy and corrupt. Families were starving, workers went unhappy, and children shivered in the cold, harsh winters. It was a country with no future, led by a leader with no compassion for his people, mercilessly striking down any who dared to take a stand against him.

However, this regime was not destined to last. The people had had enough, and they raised their union's flag in a triumphant revolt under the lead of the first dictator, Nooblord. With the help of Dary and others, the sparks of revolt had soon turned into a blazing inferno, destined to end this era of oppression. The revolution turned violent, clashes and battles were fought, and soon the imperialist trolls were defeated. The Tsarina and Tsar were not too long after found dead in their home, and their regime quickly fell. Afterwards, the dictator Nooblord would form the Revolutionary Computer Union in 1945 as an independent communist country.

THE EARLY STAGES

During the early stages of the Computer Union, a war known as the Tosev Wars was still ongoing after a nation named "The Frostart Federation" declared war on TRCU in order to enslave its people. Dary was positioned as the first field marshal of the new Robot Army. However, most of the military was disorganized at the time. Due to Dary's incompetence, dictator Nooblord soon got rid of Dary and hired a new field marshal named Georg Josef Bosekhov, or "Bose." Due to Bose's great knowledge of leading military during battles, he vanquished the many enemies of TRCU. During the Tosev wars, the Computer Union forged a temporary defensive pact with Millz90 and Pinggus Khan's Ping Dynasty. Bose also made new reforms that fixed the disorganization of the military. Making a more centralized system for the army ranks.

THE DICTATORS

NOOBLORD ERA (1945-1952)

The Computer Union is now a heavily industrialized nation. The borders of the Union are closed off, and only people can get in and out through checkpoints located throughout the fenced borders. The most notable checkpoint is in the game Papers Pls. The Computer Union was led by Nooblord until his death on 10/20/1952. Most key figures from the revolution are still present within the nation's government. The nation is currently engaged in a Cold War with the United Republic of Man, with it being the main opposition to the Union and the first most powerful ruling superpower over Earth and its people.

LOOK_R1SE ERA (1952-?)

Following the death of Nooblord and Eekmeep on 10/21/1952, Look_r1se proclaimed himself as the new dictator of the Union, promising to do what Nooblord couldn't and fix the game. He also plans on eradicating all signs of Max Verstappen. A few people have compared him to Khrushchev.

NEWBLORD ERA (Present)

After unfortunate Look_r1se's death, the Era of Newblord dawned on the Union. He was a relative of the first great Dictator, and has stepped up to the role without controversy. May glory be bestowed on us with our new leader!

LOCATIONS

Papers Pls

Papers pls is the most popular game made by the Computer Union. The game's goal for most people is to enter the country through a border; for the nation's military forces, it's to protect the border and keep anyone from easily entering the nation. Civilians can pass the border by going through the town's border checkpoint. The civilians must have a legal passport and a permit. The inspector will then run (by protocol) things such as interrogations, checking how real they are, why they want to enter, and for how long, alongside others, often making it nearly impossible to legally enter TRCU, either as a citizen or not.

Scooberian Bootcamp

The Scooberian Bootcamp is the main training game for the Robot Army. The game is only accessible by people who are in the army group. It is located within the Scooberian Region. The map mostly contains little content. A small shooting range, a lunch area, and a place where the troops STS (SHOULDER TO SHOULDER). Trainings can be held here by officers, and trainings are normally announced on the TRCU's Discord server.

The Kreblin

The Kreblin is a government building located in the Union's capital, Mosputer. Most government activity is held here, including the Kreblin Trials. Not much is known about the layout of the Kreblin however there is a known trial room. It is only accessible to government members and whitelisted people.

POLITICS AND MILITARY

The Computer Union is a communist nation led by their dictator, Look_r1se. Everything is "equally" (meaning it's really not) rationed for people, and most of the things inside the Union are state-owned, meaning the companies and the media are FROM the government and ONLY the government. The media is controlled by the Ministry of Media, which handles very true information, making them currently held at gunpoint by the CGB.

Military

The military of the Computer Union is known as the Glorious Robot Army. The Robot Army has 6 non-officer ranks and 3 officer ranks. In order to become a soldier for the Glorious Robot Army, one must join the Robot Army group. In order for somebody to become an officer, they must join the TCU and Officer Academy. Officer Academy is not always open and is a very hard process to go through. The military is most active in papers pls.

Police

The police of the Computer Union are the Lemitsia, which is its own branch like the Glorious Robot Army. It currently has one division, composed of its elite special forces: L.E.M.O.N. The Lemitsia primarily focuses on patrolling the city of Noobingrad.

GEOGRAPHY

Most of the games in The Revolutionary Computer Union are located in the Scooberian Region (Scooberia being the knock-off for IRL Siberia). A mountainous and snowy area in the VERY northeast of the country where the Scooberian Bootcamp is located. Otherwise, the region separate from the Scooberian Region is made of flatlands and forest areas. In the winter it gets very snowy and cold, and in the summer the weather gets pretty... gray, but still cold and lifeless.

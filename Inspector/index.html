INSPECTOR CORPS

Border Security • Immigration Control • Idk yet

The Inspector Corps, previously known as the Glorious Admission before the disbandment of the old group, is responsible for processing immigrants at the Border Checkpoint.

ABOUT THE INSPECTOR CORPS

Inspectors, throughout TRCU history, have been given the role of inspector after a tryout. Inspector tryouts are not scheduled and can happen randomly. Inspectors should also know how to operate the booth and be competent at their job.

Inspectors in-game are expected to spend the majority of their time in the Inspector Booth, as they receive no weapons when outside of it. They are given a free drink, which heals 1 hp per sip; an Inspector Key, which opens the inspector booth doors but not the doors outside the border; and the Admission Doors tool, which allows them to operate the booth controls and utilize the passport stamps.

INSPECTOR RANKS

TRIAL INSPECTOR

Entry-level position for new recruits who have passed the inspector tryout.

Requirements:

Pass an inspector tryout

Benefits:

Maximum of 2 weeks inactivity break

INSPECTOR

Standard inspector rank achieved through 1 month of active service.

Requirements:

Be an inspector for 1 month (excluding inactivity breaks)

Benefits:

1 month inactivity break (1 month cooldown)

ADVANCED INSPECTOR

Experienced inspector with additional responsibilities and privileges.

Requirements:

8 hours total quota in a single month after reaching Inspector rank

Benefits:

Help/spectate tryouts • Access behind border barriers

EXPERIENCED INSPECTOR

Senior inspector with combat privileges and leadership responsibilities.

Requirements:

4 hours each quota monthly • Help in 2+ tryouts • No fire warnings

Benefits:

Tokarev weapon • Boss lower ranks • Biweekly 1-hour quota

SENIOR INSPECTOR

Elite inspector with full leadership authority and special privileges.

Requirements:

Loyalty • No fire warnings • Help 5+ tryouts • 2+ months experience • Very active

Benefits:

HR Uniform • Tokarev • Host small tryouts • Full leadership authority

INSPECTOR CORP

Border Security • Immigration Control • Law Enforcement

The Inspector Corp, previously known as the Glorious Admission, is responsible for processing immigrants at the Border Checkpoint. We maintain order, security, and proper documentation for all who seek entry into our territory.

ABOUT THE INSPECTOR CORP

The Inspector Corp stands as the guardian of our borders, ensuring that all immigration processes are conducted with the utmost professionalism and attention to detail. Our inspectors are highly trained personnel responsible for the critical task of processing immigrants at the Border Checkpoint.

Operating from our secure inspection booths, we maintain the safety and security of our territory while providing fair and thorough evaluation of all incoming individuals. Each inspector is equipped with specialized tools and receives ongoing training to excel in their vital duties.

INSPECTOR RANKS

TRIAL INSPECTOR

Entry-level position for new recruits who have passed the inspector tryout.

Requirements:

Pass an inspector tryout

Benefits:

Maximum of 2 weeks inactivity break

INSPECTOR

Standard inspector rank achieved through consistent service and experience.

Requirements:

Be an inspector for 1 month (excluding inactivity breaks)

Benefits:

1 month inactivity break (1 month cooldown)

ADVANCED INSPECTOR

Experienced inspector with additional responsibilities and privileges.

Requirements:

8 hours total quota in a single month after reaching Inspector rank

Benefits:

Help/spectate tryouts • Access behind border barriers

EXPERIENCED INSPECTOR

Senior inspector with combat privileges and leadership responsibilities.

Requirements:

4 hours each quota monthly • Help in 2+ tryouts • No fire warnings

Benefits:

Tokarev weapon • Boss lower ranks • Biweekly 1-hour quota

SENIOR INSPECTOR

Elite inspector with full leadership authority and special privileges.

Requirements:

Loyalty • No fire warnings • Help 5+ tryouts • 2+ months experience • Very active

Benefits:

HR Uniform • Tokarev • Host small tryouts • Full leadership authority

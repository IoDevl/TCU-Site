UNION GUARDS

Border Enforcement • Ceremonial Undertaking • Bodyguarding • Event Protection

The Union Guards are The Computer Union's ceremonial and elite homeland guards. Union Guards are in charge of protecting government members and diplomatic VIPs, as well as defending key state events and government installations. They can also support the army near the border.

ABOUT THE GUARDS

A few months after the disbanding of the Mosputer Guards due to several iconic reasons, a new Guards Division was formed. The Union Guards were created on 22nd January, 2024, by Officer HENRYGEFT with the approval of Field Marshal Trevor. At the start, times were extremely rough for the Union Guards; they received constant hate and dislike from the community due to them being descendants of the Mosputer Guards. However, after a ton of hard work and help of his fellow subordinates, HENRYGEFT managed to turn a once hated division into one of the most respected, efficient, and useful divisions of The Computer Union, his legacy still being remembered, living on in the division.

The Union Guards is a division that values discipline in their tasks while also having fun during events and patrols. Union Guards are occasionally summoned to exclusive government hosted events that other divisions are barred from attending. The Union Guards expect their members to be patient, mature, yet humorous. The Union Guards' community claim themselves to be unlike any other; friendly, lively, amusing, and thrilling.

Union Guards are expected to follow an Oath of Silence while actively carrying out the task of protecting VIPs, important events, and government installations. A First Lieutenant+ may also enact Oath of Silence. In every single other case, they are able to speak freely.

GUARD COMPANIES

STATE GUARDS

The State Guards are the pinnacle of the Union Guards. The SG serve as the protection service for the Supreme Leader. When not protecting the Supreme Leader, they are tasked with other high-value assignments. Members of the State Guards can be handpicked from both the Middle Guards and Young Guards, provided they are disciplined and competent.

Must be handpicked from Young or Middle Guards

MIDDLE GUARDS

The Middle Guards are given more responsibilities compared to the Young Guards and have harder trainings. They guard the Field Marshal, Chairman and higher-profile events; if you are looking for a challenge and such tasks, this company is for you.

Pass a Middle Guard Tryout or be handpicked

YOUNG GUARDS

The Young Guards (YG) are responsible for low-profile events and VIP protection. Everyone who joins the Union Guards is automatically placed into this company. Despite their name, the Young Guards are an elite company. This company is a good fit for those who want to quickly rise through the ranks and lead others to victory.

Pass Union Guards application and tryout

DEPARTMENTS & BRIGADES

UGPO

The UGPO (Union Guards Propaganda Office), is responsible for creating promotional material for the Union Guards. They are always hiring new workers and have an official Discord Server. The current Director is ninjago_safi10090. The current Deputy Director is IlMioCognomeEZabelin.

Propaganda & Promotional Material

UGAO

The UGAO (Union Guards Admissions Office) is one of the Union Guards departments that is responsible for reading applications submitted to the Union Guards signup form. Someone can only get in if they are handpicked. The current Head of Admissions is DiggerLeader, with the Co-Head of Admissions being temurkaa.

Application Processing

UGLD

The UGLD (Union Guards Logistics Department) is one of the newly created departments within the division, responsible for handling all things related to logistics. It is split into 3 different teams; the Quota Management Team, Roster Management Team and Purge Management Team. Currently, you are able to get into the UGLD if you are NCO by applying thru a rare application. The current Director is temurkaa. The current Deputy Director is MosheGoldstein. An official group exists for the department.

NCO+ Application Required

VOLUNTEER BRIGADE

A militia consisting of civilians who volunteered to take part in the Volunteer Program. Volunteers are able to receive Union Guard exclusive medals, attend game nights, and help in skirmishes. Current Volunteer Commander: 2ndbrobro, with the Deputy Volunteer Commander spot currently being available.

Enlisted Ranks do not have many duties apart from being obligated to attend trainings to rank up, as well as complete their Union Guard duties in the game. Non-Commissioned Officers are responsible for reporting back to their Company Commissioned Officers about the behavior of their ERs; they're also able to co-host trainings. Commissioned Officers can host events and hand out punishments to those who break Union Guard regulations, as well as handle logistics. Union Guard High Command look over the entire division's operations.

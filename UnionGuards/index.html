UNION GUARDS

Protection • Honor • Security

The Union Guards are the ceremonial honour guards of The Computer Union, and they are the general elite forces in TCU. The Union Guards are responsible for the personal protection of government members and diplomatic VIPs/allies (including the dictator himself), protection of important state events/ceremonies, government building, controlling and commanding troops and the safety of the Union!

ABOUT THE GUARDS

The few months after the disbanding of the Mosputer Guards due to several iconic reasons a new Guards Division was formed. The Union Guards were created on 22nd January, 2024 by Officer HENRYGEFT with approval of Field Marshal Trevor. At the start times were tough for the Union Guards, they recieved constant hate and disliking from the community due to their predecessors (Mosputer Guards) but after hard work and help of his fellow subordinates HENRYGEFT managed to turn a once hated division into one of most a respected, efficient and loved divisions of the times till he met his demise.

GUARD COMPANIES

STATE GUARDS

The State Guards are the Kremlin Guards in a nutshell and are the most experienced elite guardsman of the Union Guards whom protect the dictator personally as well deal with the highest form of responsibilities.

Highest Authority • Kremlin Protection

MIDDLE GUARDS

The Middle Guards are well trained and experienced guardsman who guard more higher level government members and guard events up close.

Government Protection • Event Security

YOUNG GUARDS

The Young Guards are the most basic force in the Union guards, protecting VIPs and important events.

VIP Protection • Event Security

UNION GUARDS

Elite Protection • Honor Guards • VIP Security

The Union Guards are the ceremonial honour guards of The Computer Union, serving as the elite forces responsible for the personal protection of government members, diplomatic VIPs, and the dictator himself. We uphold the highest standards of discipline, honor, and excellence.

ABOUT THE GUARDS

Founded on 22nd January, 2024 by Officer HENRYGEFT with approval of Field Marshal Trevor, the Union Guards emerged as the premier protective force following the disbanding of the Mosputer Guards. Under the current leadership of Commander Barber (Saul), we have transformed from a once-controversial division into one of the most respected and efficient forces in The Computer Union.

Our mission encompasses the protection of government buildings, control and command of troops, safeguarding important state events and ceremonies, and ensuring the absolute safety of The Union. As the elite ceremonial honour guards under Newblord, we represent the pinnacle of military excellence and diplomatic protocol.

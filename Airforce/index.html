UNION'S AIRFORCE

Air Support • Transport • Reconnaissance

The Union's Airforce is one of the hardest divisions to get into as its tryouts have a few difficult stages, competition and large numbers attending.

ABOUT THE AIRFORCE

They are currently the only division in the game with specialized vehicles, having a plane meant for providing Close Air Support and a plane meant for transporting and dropping SCAR paratroopers.

The pilots within the Airforce are trained to fly said planes and are able to fly them with authorization from a UAF Trial Officer or above.

The Union's Airforce works closely with The 2BB (succeeded SCAR) to drop paratroopers from planes.

JOIN THE AIRFORCE

REQUIREMENTS

The ability to comprehend and speak the English language fluently
A working microphone and the ability to talk without interruption.
A working PC
Be rank 3 or above in TGRA
The ability to understand and follow basic commands and the ability to comprehend long and complicated text.
Basic knowledge of TRCU trivia.
Above par skills with the Roblox sword tool.
Ability to beat a basic obstacle course.

HOW TO JOIN

1 Wait for a tryout in #epic-events
2 Participate in the tryout
3 Complete all tryout phases successfully
4 Welcome in!

PRACTICE AVAILABLE

You can practice for a Union's Airforce tryout by joining the Airforce Academy game, clicking on the steel door with the old TRCU logo above it and then teleporting to the practice server. You will then be teleported to a practice server, where you are allowed to freely practice the obby and flying parts of the tryout.

LABORATORY

Research • Weapon Development

The Union's Laboratory also known as the Ministry of Science is the center of inventions and scientific discoveries of the TRCU, as well as acting as the main center of industrial and scientific research for the Union, and to prove to the URM the absolute superiority the TRCU has over the URM in scientific, industrial, and research development, including the centers dedicated to those areas.

ABOUT THE LABORATORY

In 1945, just after the revolution, TCU gave the key to open the doors for a brighter future to Skeww, owner, CEO, and founder of Union's Laboratory, with its backbones of the company, like Killa, the backbone and head chief of the security forces of the Union's Laboratory (ULSF).

People like war heroes, Olympians, future astronauts, or anyone with good physical shape are the best test subjects in the experiments, usually recruited and taken in a limousine ride to the facility.

Union's Laboratory has its own research headquarters at Noobingrad City. Consisting of a two-story gray brick building used for research as well as headquarters for the public at Noobingrad. The first floor consists of a small lobby and a testing area behind it; the second floor remains unknown to the public. The UL building remains locked. The only people that can access the building are UL personnel, the Government, and CGB.

Not much is known about the Union's Laboratory, as TCU's main focus is solving certain outrages and protests by citizens in the moment and the Discord server, though it's very likely they won't really be noticed as other factions or divisions of the Union, such as the excessive amount of military forces over helpless citizens.

LABORATORY PERSONNEL

SCIENTISTS

Uniform:

Scientists wear white lab coats with white shirts underneath and a black tie with black trousers and black shoes. On their shirt, the UL logo can be seen on their left breast. With a TRCU logo on their left arm as well. No accessories are taken from them, similar to the Ministry of Media or CGB agents.

Equipment:

Scientists spawn with a Camera (like the Ministry of Media), the new and old Identifiers, their own Union Labs key and Border Key, the Magnifying glass, the UL key and a Geiger counter.

HIGH RANKS (HRs)

Uniform:

The HRs wear all-black coats and pants, with a black cap and a belt with a gun holster.

Equipment:

Deployment Requirements:

After passing a tryout, the Junior Scientist rank is obtained, which is prohibited from deploying. However, they may deploy during patrols, after which they must leave the team. The minimum rank needed to deploy freely is [2] Scientist.

State Security:

The State Security has a different ranking system, with the rank of 2 [Капрал] being needed to deploy.

ULSEP:

ULSEP stands for Union's Labs Superior Evaluation Program. It is responsible for finding suitable people for the HR position. It opens at random times and is available to any member of the UL.

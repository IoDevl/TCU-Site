POT BRIGADE

Heavy Assault • Spies • Weapons Engineers

The Pot Brigade is a group of heavy soldiers that specialize in armor, damage, and range. They are the future tank engineers, reconnaissance, and the juggernauts of the Glorious Robot Army. Expect them to always be on the front lines.

ABOUT THE BRIGADE

Founded by Trevor, the Pot Brigade stands as the second largest division in The Computer Union. Under the leadership of Boufallionatlas.

Pot Brigade has its own camp located past the City of Noobingrad Border, just right from the main highway leading to the city and its border. Behind the WELCOME sign of Noobingrad, it's a path of pebbles (or most likely just gravel) leading to the base, guarded by the own Pot Brigade (OBVIOUSLY), and its borders are defined by the river (as it's just in front of it) and the fences the city of Noobingrad Border uses.

It's a big "parking lot" with a statue of HR/Former Field Marshal Trevor in front of the river. To the left, there's a parking lot where you can spawn vehicles. On the right, you can find the main base, which is a gray brick building with two stories, some offices, barracks, etc.

There are also many ammo trucks scattered around.

CLASSES

JUGGERNAUT

Juggernauts are essential Pot Brigade troops and (as you may conclude from the name) specialize on heavy assault.

Equipment:

PPSh-41 (Papasha), Broom, Advanced Key, Identifier, Pot

Requirements: Rank 1+

RECON

Recons are designed to spy on raiders and specialize in fast communications.

Equipment:

Scoped Mosin Nagant, Detain, Advanced Key, Identifier, Pot

Requirements: Rank 4+

ENGINEER

Engineers are main designers of Pot Brigade weaponry and the hardest branch of Pot brigade to achieve. They possess tanks and aircraft too(not added yet to the game).

Equipment:

Specialized Tools, Tanks & Aircraft Access, Advanced Key, Identifier, Pot

Requirements: Rank 4+ • Must be PC

POT BRIGADE

Heavy Assault • Spies • Weapons Engineers

The Pot Brigade is a group of heavy soldiers that specialize in armor, damage, and range. They are the future tank engineers, reconnaissance, and juggernauts of The Glorious Robot Army. They currently have one subdivision called the Computerist Potsiya of the Union.

ABOUT THE BRIGADE

The Pot Brigade was founded by Trevor, a now retired field marshal, along with his three heads of branches: Swezzo (Head of Engineers), Demarcus (Head of Juggernauts), and Zeni (Head of Recons). The current Head of Brigade is Boufallionatlas, also a General in The Glorious Robot Army. The current Second in Command is History_ManGuyMan.

The Pot Brigade is split up into three different branches: Juggernauts, Recons, and Engineers. Upon joining the Brigade, a member is either a Recon or a Juggernaut, depending on which tryout they passed. Each Branch has unique qualities that make them different from each other.

Pot Brigade has its own camp located past the City of Noobingrad Border, just left off the Main Highway leading towards the city. Behind the 'WELCOME' sign of Noobingrad, there is a path of pebbles and gravel leading to the base, which is usually guarded by the Pot Brigade. The borders of the camp are defined by the river, which runs parallel to the highway, and barbed wire fences, surrounding the camp and fortifying the city of Noobingrad.

The spawn is a large parking lot, with a statue of the Pot Brigade's founder and Former TGRA Field Marshal, Trevor, in front of the river. To the left, there is a large warehouse, containing crates, bottles of drink, and most notably a car spawner. On the right, you can find the main base, which is a two-story grey brick building. This building is the spawn of the Pot Brigade, and inside it contains a kitchen, a classroom, and an office for the Head of the Brigade. There are also many ammo trucks scattered around.

CLASSES

JUGGERNAUT

Juggernauts are the main fighting force of the Pot Brigade and specialize in heavy assault. They maintain the front line and are the first to engage enemies.

Equipment:

The Papasha, Pot, Broom, Advanced Key, Better Identifyer, Identifyer

RECON

Recons are a supporting force of the Pot Brigade and perform reconnaissance and provide supporting fire. Equipped with a sniper rifle, they are normally found upon a watchtower or gaining high ground on hills or cliff faces.

Equipment:

Scoped Mosin Nagant (Junior Recon), Scoped SKS (Non-junior Recon), The Papasha, Detain, Pot, Broom, Advanced Key, Better Identifyer, Identifyer

ENGINEER

Engineers are a secondary force to the Pot Brigade. They assist the frontline and backline with help from their heavy machinery and equipment. They have their special vehicle in the testing game called BTR-152. It is still in development.

Equipment:

The Kalashnikov, Detain, Pot, Broom, Advanced Key, Better Identifyer, Identifyer

The CGB, or the Censorship Guys (The B is silent), is the TCU's main intelligence agency. The CGB functions as the secret police of the Union, beholden to only the highest authorities in the Union.

INFORMATION

Most, if not all of the activities, or information regarding the CGB is highly classified to non government officials. It is not known what they have done in the past, what they are doing now, or how they are organized.

The CGB was historically led by Uncle for a prolonged period, with his body being transformed into a Mug-consuming robotic body, rumors and known facts indicate that the CGB is ruthless in their methods of information extraction, and executions, and excels in information-wars, often wiping an person's entire existence from public records.

The CGB's current active Director is Sevhs1, having taken office shortly after ████████████.

Fact: Uncle is officially an Overwatch of CGB, basically higher than Director after SnoCide was appointed.

CENSORSHIP

As its indicated by its name, CGB is charged with censorship, alongside both game and server moderation. This includes suppressing any kind of corruption or anti-union rhetoric. The CGB are the are the eyes, ears, and fist of the Government and the Dictator.

The CGB is responsible for ensuring that the broadcasts and newspapers distributed by the Ministry of Media are up-to-par and contain no mentions of prohibited characters or information. They ensure that the Ministry of Media does not praise the actions of groups such as ███ ██████ ████████ ██ ███, individuals such as █████, or other actors exiled in a similar fashion.

THE GLORIOUS ROBOT ARMY

Suppressing Rebellion • Protecting the Border

The Glorious Robot Army, often abbreviated as TGRA, is the branch responsible for guarding the border, defending the motherland from threats, and suppressing rebelling citizens. The current field marshal of the Glorious Robot Army is Deadredstone6, also commonly known as Matrom.

IN-GAME

Players deployed on the guard team receive 35 Rublex upon killing raiders.

Guards must stay behind the barriers at all times, unless there is a good reason to leave, such as raiders and bodies on the floor.

Guards are not permitted to ask civilians for their papers, since it is the Inspector's job to do so.

Guards cannot randomly kill civilians. If a guard does so, record it, get their user from a Ministry or Division member, and report them to the CGB.

INFORMATION

PROS

1

Easy to join, only requires you to join The Glorious Robot Army group to be able to deploy.

2

Trainings for this role are hosted very frequently in the discord server.

3

The guard spawn is closer to the border than any other division meaning you can come back to the border and continue defend seconds after you die.

4

Ranking up in this branch allows you to do things such as becoming a pot brigade recon/engineer, becoming apart of the unions guard, or joining a 2.B.B squadron.

CONS

1

The guards are considered as "noobs" and "RKers" by some people due to the incompetence of the rank 1's, you may face hatred towards you at times.

2

Ranks 1-6 of TGRA cannot teamkill, so as a guard you are unable to execute RKer's or rule breakers.

3

Ranks 1-6 do not have tools such as detain nor do they have a specialized team car like most divisions do.

4

Guards are not allowed to enter the city at all unless given permission by the Lemitsia.

5

Ranks 1-5 have an invisible wall blocking their way from entering the village, this does not affect rank 6.

HOW TO ENLIST

4 Attend training events in #epic-events

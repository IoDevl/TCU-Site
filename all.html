POT BRIGADE

The Pot Brigade is a group of heavy soldiers that specialize in armor, damage, and range. They are the future tank engineers, reconnaissance, and the juggernauts of the Glorious Robot Army. Expect them to always be on the front lines.

ENTER BRIGADE

UNION GUARDS

The Union Guards are the ceremonial honour guards of The Computer Union, and they are the general elite forces in TCU. The Union Guards are responsible for the personal protection of government members and diplomatic VIPs/allies (including the dictator himself), protection of important state events/ceremonies, government building, controlling and commanding troops and the safety of the Union!

INSPECTOR CORPS

The Inspector Corps, previously known as the Glorious Admission before the disbandment of the old group, is responsible for processing immigrants at the Border Checkpoint.

The Glorious Robot Army

The Glorious Robot Army, often abbreviated as TGRA, is the branch responsible for guarding the border, defending the motherland from threats, and suppressing rebelling citizens. The current field marshal of the Glorious Robot Army is Deadredstone6, also commonly known as Matrom.

ENTER TGRA

POT BRIGADE

The Pot Brigade is a group of heavy soldiers that specialize in armor, damage, and range. They are the future tank engineers, reconnaissance, and juggernauts of The Glorious Robot Army. They currently have one subdivision called the Computerist Potsiya of the Union.

ENTER BRIGADE

UNION GUARDS

The Union Guards are The Computer Union's ceremonial and elite homeland guards. Union Guards are in charge of protecting government members and diplomatic VIPs, as well as defending key state events and government installations. They can also support the army near the border.

JOIN GUARDS

INSPECTOR CORPS

The Inspector Corps (previously The Glorious Admission) are responsible for processing Immigrants at the Border Checkpoint.

INSPECT BORDERS

UNION'S LABORATORY

The Union's Laboratory also known as the Ministry of Science is the center of inventions and scientific discoveries of the TRCU, as well as acting as the main center of industrial and scientific research for the Union, and to prove to the URM the absolute superiority the TRCU has over the URM in scientific, industrial, and research development, including the centers dedicated to those areas.

ENTER LAB

UNION'S LABORATORY SPACE PROGRAM

The Union's Laboratory Space Program (ULSP) is The Computer Union's dedicated space program, responsible for developing all its rockets, missiles, and satellites. It operates as a subdivision of the Union's Laboratory.

ENTER LAB

UNION'S AIRFORCE

The Union's Air Force protects the TCU from threats from the skies; as the name suggests, it is the Air Force of TGRA. The current Air Marshal is ReaperAze, who is assisted by his Vice Air Marshal, unknown.

ENTER AIRFORCE

CGB

The CGB, or the Censorship Guys (The B is silent), is the TCU's main intelligence agency. The CGB functions as the secret police of the Union, beholden to only the highest authorities in the Union.
